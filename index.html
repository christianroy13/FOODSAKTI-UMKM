PT Food Sakti Emas is an Indonesian food company specializing in multipurpose and ready-to-use seasonings made from natural spices. Founded with a mission to make cooking easier and tastier, we create authentic Indonesian flavors to homes and businesses worldwide.

Our certificate

We have several legal documents for MSMEs that support export activities.

01

Certificate CPPOB

A CPPOB Certificate is an official document issued by an authorized authority confirming that a company applies Good Processed Food Manufacturing Practices in producing and handling food products.

02

Certificate Halal

A Halal Certificate is an official document issued by an authorized institution stating that a product complies with Islamic law and is permissible for consumption by Muslims.

Risk-Based Business Licensing

A Risk-Based Business Licensing Certificate is an official document issued by the government that certifies a business has obtained permission to operate according to its risk level.
